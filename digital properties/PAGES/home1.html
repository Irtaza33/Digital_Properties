Bahria Town Karachi

We Provide Construction all kinds Of Services in Bahria Town Karachi Contact us today for best services.

Bahria Town Karachiis a privately-owned gated suburb under construction near Karachi Pakistan The suburb is being developed by the Bahira Town and occupies under 46,000 acres (72 sq mi; 19,000 ha), just off the M-9 Motor Way Karachi.

The community will include the Grand Jamia Mosque, which will be the world's third-largest, and the Rafi Cricket Stadium the country's largest, designed by GMW Architects. The community is divided by the 400-foot-wide (120 m), 18-lane, Jinnah Avenue. It is also home to a 36-hole United States Golf standard golf course.
